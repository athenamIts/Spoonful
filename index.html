What is the Spoon Theory?

The Spoon Theory is a way many people with chronic illnesses, disabilities, and fatigue describe their daily energy. Each "spoon" represents a unit of energy. Some days you wake up with plenty. Other days you have every few. It is not about motivation, It is about energy and capacity.
